ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Aurose is a fashion brand conceptualized in 2024. What started as an ambitious idea has evolved into a promising fashion initiative, with plans to establish a chain of luxurious showrooms across key cities in Sri Lanka. While still in its early stages, Aurose aspires to become a leading name in the fashion industry, focusing on modern elegance and sophistication.

With a future vision of growing a dedicated workforce, Aurose aims to bring together passionate individuals to help redefine the fashion retail landscape in Sri Lanka. Though our journey has just begun, we are committed to offering a curated range of clothing, cosmetics, accessories, and homeware that caters to diverse tastes and lifestyles.

At Aurose, we believe in blending affordability with luxury, creating a shopping experience that is stylish, accessible, and inspiring. Our goal is to bring fashion enthusiasts quality products that exude glamour and grace, making each visit an extraordinary experience. As we look ahead, Aurose strives to set new standards in the fashion world, focusing on growth, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Vision

To redefine Sri Lankan fashion by blending sophistication, affordability, and sustainability, empowering authentic self-expression.

Mission

Our mission at Aurose is to craft an unparalleled shopping experience that celebrates diversity, creativity, and quality. We are committed to curating a collection that combines modern trends with enduring elegance while prioritizing ethical sourcing and customer satisfaction. Through innovation and dedication, we aim to inspire confidence and self-expression in every customer we serve.
